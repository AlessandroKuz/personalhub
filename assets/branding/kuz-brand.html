05 — Usage Guidelines

Do

  • Dark backgrounds (#0a0a0a, #111111) are the primary context
  • Wide letter-spacing (0.28–0.35em) for all wordmark applications
  • Use the K avatar for sizes below 200px wide
  • Ice accent lives exclusively on the hairline rule
  • Maintain clear space equal to the cap-height of "K" on all sides
  • Use Jost 300 for all running body text

Don't

  • Use the ice colour on the letterforms themselves
  • Place the wordmark below 120px wide
  • Apply the mark on mid-grey or photographic backgrounds
  • Use ice on more than one element per composition
  • Add shadows, glows, or outlines to any letterform
  • Substitute Jost — the pure geometry is the brand DNA
